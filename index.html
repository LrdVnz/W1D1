Focaccia with Caramellized Onions, Cranberries, and Brie

Wow your taste with this festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramellized onions, tart cranberries, and cream brie. It will become your new favorite holiday starter!

Image : Sally Vegas

FEATURED IN:

Fresh Cranberries Recipes That Go Beyond Sauce

INGREDIENTS

For the focaccia:

  • 4 cups(500g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 3/4 cups(350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil, plus more for the pan

For the topping:

  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoons olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes (scrape rind off with a paring knife if desired, though it's not necessary)
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries
